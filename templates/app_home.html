GPA

GPA is the abbrevation for Grade Point Average which is typicallly calculated by adding all the numbered grades you have received and dividing them by the number of credits you have taken from semester exams.

CGPA

CGPA is defined as Cumulative Grade Points Average CGPA is the measurement of average grade points obtained by a student in all the semesters.
